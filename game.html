Aim of the game

This game is all about of guessing the capital of the different countries. You have 20 countries where you need to choose which is the capital of the respective country. The game test your knowledge about geographical information that you have and if you don't have it to learn about it.

How to play the game

Press play to start the game board

You can probably guess the capitals of France and Italy, but how well would you do if you had to come up with capitals of different countries?

Let's test it now!

Successfully complete 20 rounds to win the game and be notified of your point that you would take for each good answer and you would be able to put your name on the highscore list.
